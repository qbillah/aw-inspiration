MY FIRST:

MUSICAL MEMORY

My dad bought me a toy tape deck and cassette. The cassette had one song on it that I'd have on repeat all day long. The song was called 'Coffee House' by Bengali singer/songwritter Manna Dey

MY PARENTS & MUSIC

My parents have always encouraged me to listen to music and be open to things I've never heard before. I think music is so inspirational to me because of them. My mom used to practice singing when she was younger and my dad is just a huge fan of music.

MUSIC & ME

Music inspires me everyday and is a very important part of my daily life. I think the perfect song can make anything happen. It might help spark an idea or make you feel great after a terrible day. When you're listening to a song you're listening to someone's feelings and energy. There's a connection there which I find to be quite inspiring.
